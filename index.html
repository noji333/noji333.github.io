Whats New?

Faster then the speed of light!: Since the release of imadejptr, it has always been a rushed state because not only was it rushed to people, it was also not really thought out in design. The more that was added to the site, the more slower the site was. For V2, we made sure to speed up loading times and games too, making playing games on imadejptr easier then ever. More Customization!: We have heard A LOT of complaints about the colors not being easy on the eyes. So, we made themes a priority when making V2. With a wide variety of themes to choose from, you can change the look of the website with just a click of a button! New Systems!: We wanted to add something new to the website that adds more possiblities of what can be played on a game site. So we decided to add four new systems here on imadejptr! The Atari 2600, Sega Master System, Sega 32X, Sega Game Gear, and Playstation 1 are now all avaliable on IMJ V2! Ease of access: No more cluster navigation bars. Instead, we offer a much easier way to access the games that you want to play. We want people from all over the internet to be able to access this website with ease and were hoping this will help the cause!
